For the love of the Music #01

2014-09-19

The very first episode of For the Love of the Music-series. (I'm planning to do a lot more of these.) Again mainly Deep House, but also some Indie Dance / Nu Disco tracks.

Personally I think that this is best DJ-set I've done so far.
